Third party inspection Company

A third-party inspection (TPI) is when an independent company, not involved in either the manufacturing or the selling, ensures that the supply chain runs smoothly by conducting a product inspection for quality control.They’ll send out the inspection team to the factory to conduct quality inspections and conduct a series of testing on the products to check if they meet the required quality standards
